Remotely Monitored Hybrid Renewable Energy System

UBC Sustaingineering | 2022-2024

Overview

Having joined UBC Sustaingineering's Electrical team in my first year at UBC Engineering, I grew alongside the team throughout my degree. The Hybrid Renewable Energy System (HRES) is a combined wind and solar powered capture and storage system designed for the team's overall offgrid Sustainable Mobile Research Testbed (SMRT) project. As the Electrical team's co-lead, I helped manage the project by communicating with other subteams (such as building design) and stakeholders on their needs as well as with management and suppliers regarding budgets and timelines.
