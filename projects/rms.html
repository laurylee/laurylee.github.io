Remote Monitoring System

UBC Sustaingineering | 2020-2021

Overview

In my first year with UBC Sustaingineering's Electrical team, we worked with a non-profit organization in Nicaragua to provide a monitoring solution for solar-powered water pumps in remote farm areas. The system was easily integrable into existing pumps and used the 3G cellular network through the Particle microcontroller to communicate the operational levels. A PCB was used to step down the high-voltage solar array input of 300V to a 5V level and connected peripheral sensors to the main micrcontroller. C++ and object-oriented programming was used to maintain modularity throughout.

Lauryn Lee 2025
